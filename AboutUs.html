About us

Eat Die You restaurant combines an inspired fusion menu from across Europe with a casually modern style setting within the heart of Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.
Eat Die You is the creation of Chef Sheng Zhong and reflects his passion for cooking with a diverse fusion menu.

"If you want to become a great chef, you have to work with great chefs.

And that's exactly what I did."

Sheng Zhong, Executive Chef/Owner

Sheng Zhong has partnered with co-owner Nicholas to open this restaurant, the two have a long history growing up together in a small town Cheras.
The menu takes its inspiration from classic Europe dishes like beef, lamb, pork and seafoods.

Eat Die You restaurant is located within the Fraser Business Park building dating from 2006, creating a sense of modern features.

An olive tree greets you at the entrance of the restaurant, a wallpaper with birds and plants and a modern lighting to bright up the restaurant.


Our Mission and Vision

To provide a modern feeling to the customers and a wide variety of food across Europe.
To be the most affordable modern restaurant in Malaysia.
